JMK Regency Hotel

Welcome to JMK Regency Hotel, your ultimate destination for luxury, comfort, and affordability in the spiritual city of Varanasi. Conveniently located at J 12/11 A, Ramkatora, near Yes Bank/Kotak Mahindra Bank, Varanasi-221001, our hotel is the ideal choice for business travelers, vacationers, and pilgrims alike.

Our meticulously designed accommodations include Standard and Deluxe Rooms, crafted to provide a serene retreat after a long day of exploration or work. Each room is equipped with modern amenities such as high-speed Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, comfortable bedding, and air conditioning, ensuring an unparalleled stay.

Dining at JMK Regency is an experience in itself, with our in-house restaurant serving a variety of delectable cuisines to tantalize your taste buds. Begin your day with a complimentary breakfast and enjoy room service for a relaxed dining experience in the comfort of your room.

Our hotel is situated close to some of Varanasi's iconic attractions, including the historic Ghats, revered temples, and Sarnath—the site of Lord Buddha’s first sermon. With easy access to these cultural landmarks, we make it convenient for you to explore the rich heritage of the city.

We also pride ourselves on hosting events, from family gatherings to corporate meetings, in our well-equipped venues. Additionally, our eco-friendly practices reflect our commitment to sustainability.

At JMK Regency, our dedicated team is available 24/7 to ensure your comfort and satisfaction. We invite you to experience the best of hospitality and create unforgettable memories with us.

OUR AWESOME SERVICES

At JMK Regency Hotel, we take pride in offering exceptional services designed to make your stay as comfortable and memorable as possible. Whether you’re here for leisure, business, or a special occasion, we’ve got you covered with personalized hospitality and top-notch facilities.

Restaurant

Indulge in a variety of culinary delights at our in-house restaurant. From traditional Indian delicacies to international cuisines, we serve meals that cater to every palate. Room service is available for those who prefer dining in the comfort of their rooms.

Complimentary Wi-Fi

Stay connected with our high-speed Wi-Fi available throughout the hotel. Whether you're working remotely or sharing memories online, we ensure uninterrupted connectivity.

24/7 Front Desk Assistance

Our dedicated team at the front desk is available around the clock to assist with your queries, travel arrangements, or anything else you need to make your stay perfect.

JJMK Projects Pvt Ltd is an ISO 9001-2015 certified company, Established in 2014, is a diversified Business House headquartered in Varanasi, India. The Group’s footprints span sectors such as Civil Construction and Real Estate.
