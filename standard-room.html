ABOUT JMK REGENCY

Nestled in the heart of Varanasi at J 12/11 A, Ramkatora, Near Yes Bank/Kotak Mahindra Bank, Varanasi-221001, JMK Regency combines comfort, elegance, and affordability for all travelers. Whether you’re visiting for business, leisure, or spiritual exploration, our hotel offers a serene retreat designed to make your stay memorable.

Choose from our thoughtfully designed Standard and Deluxe Rooms, each crafted to cater to your unique preferences. From modern amenities like complimentary Wi-Fi and televisions to cozy interiors, our rooms promise relaxation and convenience throughout your stay.

Indulge in culinary delights at our in-house restaurant, where a wide variety of delectable dishes awaits to please every palate. Located close to Varanasi's iconic attractions, including the Ghats, ancient temples, and Sarnath, JMK Regency serves as the perfect base for exploring this historic city.

Our eco-conscious approach and event-hosting expertise set us apart. Be it family celebrations or corporate events, we ensure personalized service in a refined setting, making every occasion special.

With a team of friendly staff and 24/7 assistance, JMK Regency promises unparalleled hospitality. Experience the charm of Varanasi with us and create unforgettable memories.

Our hotel rooms feature free Wi-Fi and large TVs

Choose from one of the 10 spacious rooms at JMK Regency Hotel Varanasi to enjoy a relaxing stay. Our warmly decorated rooms feature convenient amenities like free Wi-Fi and a flat-screen LED TV with 270 channels. After a restful night in a plush bed with cotton linens, head to the dining area for a complimentary buffet breakfast. Before exploring nearby attractions, make a hot drink from the in-room coffee facilities or grab a cold bottle of water to take with you.
